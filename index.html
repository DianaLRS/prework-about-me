Welcome to

The Rebel Innitiative

Introduction

Our foundation aims to facilitate sexual education and access to women's health care for Mexican American women in low income regions in a culturaly aware environment.

Where We Are From

Rio Grande Valley, South Texas

What services do we provide?

We provide health education, sex education, and safe sex awareness in an open and judgement free space.

What's Oour Goal?

We want to mediate Mexican herritage and taboo topics such as sexuality, reduce teen pregnancies and STIs/STDs, and bridge the gap between culture and sex.

Where Are You Based?

RGV, South Texas.

Why do you want to help?

This is our home. We understand the difficulties and roadblocks present in this area because we experienced it ourselves. We want to give young girls and women the tools and knowledge we wish we would have had growing up.
